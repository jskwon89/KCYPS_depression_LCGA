Notes for Table 6 / Table 6b (cluster-bootstrap Cox per-wave HRs)
======================================================================
Bootstrap B = 300.
Add-one bootstrap p floor = 1/(B+1) = 1/301 = 0.00332
At 3 decimal places this rounds to .003.
Cells reading p=0.003 are at this floor; a planned supplementary B=1000 bootstrap would lower the floor to .000999.
p-values larger than the floor are exact bootstrap two-sided percentile p-values.
